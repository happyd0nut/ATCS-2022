> about me


Julia is a student, dancer, and mentor at Menlo School. Outside of classes, she enjoys building robots with her robotics team, biking around the Bay Area Peninsula, and jamming out to the bass.
Ever since her hands could hold a camera, Julia has been taking photos of her surroundings and travels. Inspired by scrapbooking at a young age, her interest for travel photography grew. Her favorite country that she's been to is Australia for its diverse wildlife and vibrant city life. As of 16-years-old, she has visited 10+ countries and 5 out of the 7 continents, taking photos of sceneries that capture moments of memories and awe.
